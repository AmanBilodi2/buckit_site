Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 20, 2025

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account info (username, email)
  • Gameplay logs (opponent, date/time, result)
  • Device and usage data (diagnostics, crashes)
  • Optional: Location (for Nearby) and Camera (for QR)—only when you enable them

How we use information

  • Provide and improve Buckit features (ratings, badges, discovery)
  • Prevent abuse and resolve disputes
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) under contract, and when required by law.

Your choices

  • Control visibility of your profile and location in Settings
  • Export or delete your data at any time via Settings → Privacy
  • Withdraw permissions in iOS Settings

Contact

Questions? Email support@buckit.app.
