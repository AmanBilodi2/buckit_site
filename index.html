Every game, every bucket

Turn pickup hoops into a real-life MyPlayer. Log 1v1s, climb a fair rating, earn badges, and find players your level at nearby parks and gyms.

Features

Create Your Player

Choose position and playstyle; your rating reflects how you hoop in real life.

Log Real Games

Search your opponent, record the result, and update both records in seconds.

Mutual Confirmation

Both players verify results to keep things fair and reduce spam.

Credibility Score

Clean history and confirmed games boost trust so your wins matter more.

Badges & Progression

Unlock and level up skill-based badges like Shot Maker, Floor General, Lockdown, and Glass Cleaner.

Discover Players & Runs

See who’s hooping near you, filter by rating, distance, or friends, and pull up to better runs.

Groups & Community

Organize games with your gym, school, or crew and keep the chat going.

Game History & Stats

Track W/L, streaks, head-to-heads, and badge progress—your hoops résumé, in your pocket.

How it works

  1. Create your player
  2. Find a nearby opponent or invite a friend
  3. Hoop, log the game, and watch your rating and badges grow
