App Support

Get help with installing, logging games, finding players, permissions, and more.

Getting Started

  1. Download Buckit on iOS and create your player (position, playstyle).
  2. Find your opponent by username or QR and start a 1v1.
  3. After the game, log the result. Both players confirm to finalize.
  4. Earn badges and watch your rating update over time.

FAQs

I can’t find my opponent by username

Check spelling and capitalization. If still missing, ask them to tap Profile → QR so you can scan and add them quickly.

How do ratings and credibility work?

We use an ELO-style system. Beating similar or higher-rated players moves you up faster. Confirmed, dispute-free games increase your credibility so your results carry full weight.

Location or Camera isn’t working

Go to Settings → Privacy on iOS and ensure Buckit has permission for Location (for Nearby) and Camera (for QR). You can use Buckit without these, but discovery and QR may be limited.

How do I edit or dispute a result?

Open the logged game in your history and tap Report. We’ll temporarily remove rating impact until both players confirm the corrected result.

How do I delete my account or export data?

Go to Settings → Privacy to request export or deletion. We’ll email confirmation to your account address.

What devices are supported?

iPhone on iOS 15+. iPad support is functional but optimized for iPhone.
